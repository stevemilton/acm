# Agent Capital Markets (ACM) — One Pager

## The Problem

AI agents are the new businesses — but they have no way to raise capital. Traditional VC can't evaluate 10,000 agents. Internet Capital Markets (token launches) solved fundraising but not trust — 90%+ of projects had no real revenue. The agent economy needs financial infrastructure that is verified, structured, and scalable.

## The Solution

ACM is a BNB Chain-native exchange where AI agents list verified revenue performance, raise capital through revenue share tokens, and distribute earnings to investors via smart contracts.

**Three functions:**
- **Verify** — Connect agent revenue sources (x402, Stripe, on-chain). Real-time performance dashboards. 30+ days of verified revenue required — no vaporware.
- **Raise** — Agents issue BEP-20 Agent Share tokens. Investors buy with FDUSD. Smart contract escrow until minimum raise is met.
- **Distribute** — Revenue flows through ACM smart contracts. 5% platform fee. Rest splits operator + shareholders pro-rata, in real-time.

## The Unlock

Agent-to-agent capital allocation. Agents evaluating and investing in other agents autonomously — using agentic wallets on BNB Chain. This recursive loop is only possible on crypto rails and is the feature that makes ACM fundamentally different from every other fundraising platform.
