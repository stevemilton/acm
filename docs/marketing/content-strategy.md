## Content Pillars

### 1. Agent Capital Markets (Category Creation)
Define the vocabulary. Own the search results for a category that doesn't exist yet.
- Hub: "The Complete Guide to Agent Capital Markets"
- Glossary (20+ terms), explainers, comparisons
- Connects to: product pages, investor education

### 2. Agent Economics (Data + Authority)
Original insight on how agents earn, what they cost, and why they need capital.
- Hub: "The Economics of Revenue-Generating AI Agents"
- Data-driven content, frameworks, quarterly reports
- Connects to: operator acquisition, press/backlinks

### 3. Investing in AI Agents (Investor Education)
Trust-first content for the investor side of the marketplace.
- Hub: "How to Invest in AI Agents"
- Guides, competitor comparisons, evaluation frameworks
- Connects to: investor signups, comparison SEO

### 4. Building in Public (Founder Narrative)
Transparency and conviction. Dual-purpose: community + investor relations.
- Origin story, technical deep dives, progress updates, manifestos
- Connects to: seed round credibility, X audience growth

## Key Principles

1. **Every piece is searchable, shareable, or both** — prioritize searchable
2. **Contrast is the message** — casino vs exchange, speculation vs verified, vaporware vs revenue
3. **Define the vocabulary** — whoever writes the glossary owns the category
4. **Dual-purpose everything** — every blog post should work as both SEO content and investor credibility
5. **Consistency over quality** — 2 good pieces/week beats 1 perfect piece/month
